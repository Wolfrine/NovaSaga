# Orders and Hostile Forms

## The Luminaries

The Luminaries are a hidden order or tradition concerned with balance, perception, and restrained intervention. Their knowledge is linked to consciousness, aura, resonance, and the quiet protection of both human settlements and hidden places.

Their charge is to maintain harmony between humanity and nature. Greed, ambition, fear, and destructive emotion are not treated only as private flaws; when amplified through people, places, objects, and crowds, they become pressures in the world.

They guide by shaping context rather than command. The preserved method uses stories, symbols, rituals, prayers, sacred places, vows, taboos, festivals, relics, and moral teachings so human minds can move toward restraint, compassion, reverence, discipline, and responsibility without being handed dangerous mechanical knowledge too early.

In the present age they appear few, regionally dispersed, and often hidden behind humble visible-world roles: healers, teachers, counselors, scholars, or quiet observers. Open display weakens their work, whether through disbelief, fear, or the social distortion that follows public power.

## Older Names

Ascendants may be an older name for the ancient discoverers or predecessors of the Luminaries. Radiant Luminaries may be a later formal name, a title, or a poetic variant.

The known Luminary categories may include city, village, field, and research forms, though no record yet proves whether these are formal orders or rough archive classifications.

## Dark Forms

Dark forces oppose balance. They are associated with [[Cosmology|negative energy]], despair, fear, corruption, and decay.

Dark beings gather strength from fear, anger, despair, and destructive emotion. They manipulate feeling, create despair-filled environments, and grow where stored negativity has already thickened the air of a place.

Dark beings may not be wholly separate from humanity. The foundation record describes corrupted human forms that can be weakened, bound, and healed rather than destroyed. The outpost tradition implied by that account preserves the human essence until balance can be restored over time.

## Unsettled Classifications

Records do not yet settle the scope of Luminary religious influence: all religions, selected symbolic traditions, later guarded practices, or mixed histories.

Extraterrestrial radiants appear in portal accounts. Their existence, terminology, and relation to Luminaries remain outside the central record.
