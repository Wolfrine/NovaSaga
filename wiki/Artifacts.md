# Charged Objects and Relics

## Objects That Hold Force

The records preserve objects that hold, amplify, or transmit energy. Their usefulness depends on more than physical form; intention, emotional state, and alignment appear to matter.

Some artifacts are energized by Luminaries. Others gather impressions slowly through contact, ritual, suffering, devotion, or repeated use.

## Relics and Sacred Forms

Sacred symbols and relics belong to the Luminary method of indirect guidance. They can hold vow, memory, grief, hope, discipline, or communal attention in forms people can approach without being told the full mechanism.

It remains unclear whether religious relics are Luminary-made, human-made, charged by collective belief, or later objects gathered into older symbolic paths.

## Harm and Healing

In balanced hands, a charged object may stabilize, heal, protect, or amplify positive resonance. In corrupted hands, the same class of object may spread fear, decay, manipulation, or imbalance.

Whether sacred symbols can become dangerous through corrupted belief or misused reverence remains untested.

## Unsettled Objects

Specific amulets, portal suits, travel aids, and saint-like relic stones are preserved in older accounts but have not entered the central record.

Some accounts make artifacts central to travel and final-war strategy. Others treat them as rare supports rather than the core of the conflict.

Ordinary human use, safe handling, rarity, and the proper test for a charged object remain unrecorded.
