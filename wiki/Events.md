# Chronology and Recorded Disturbances

## The Event

The Event is the central disturbance in current records. It disrupts electricity and weakens hidden protections, bringing the visible world and hidden realms into contact.

The conflict between Luminaries and dark forces becomes active after this disturbance.

## First Passage

Older accounts preserve the first hidden-realm contact as storm-driven and accidental. A small companion group leaves for a trek; poisonous fruit, sleep, storm, or flood carries them into danger; a river passage leads toward a weakened hidden realm.

## Preserved Sequence

The Event reaches its height, and electricity fails across the visible world. A hidden sanctuary is attacked by dark-controlled or wolf-like creatures. A Luminary intervenes.

The companions return to a collapsing public world. Their community service draws renewed Luminary attention. Training, recovery missions, artifact conflicts, and wider war may follow.

## Unsettled Chronology

The Event may be permanent, long-term, or partly reversible. Older notes lean toward permanence, but the record is not settled.

The cycle of the Event may be 3000 years. This remains an inherited claim, not an established chronology.

The final conflict may become a multi-front battle involving artifacts and allied Luminary forces. Its form remains unrecorded.
