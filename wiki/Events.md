# Chronology and Recorded Disturbances

## Known

The Event is the central disturbance in current records. It disrupts electricity and weakens hidden protections, bringing the visible world and hidden realms into contact.

The conflict between Luminaries and dark forces becomes active after this disturbance.

## Suspected Sequence

Older accounts preserve this order:

1. A small companion group leaves for a trek.
2. Poisonous fruit, sleep, storm, or flood carries them into danger.
3. A river passage leads toward a weakened hidden realm.
4. The Event reaches its height, and electricity fails across the visible world.
5. A hidden sanctuary is attacked by dark-controlled or wolf-like creatures.
6. A Luminary intervenes.
7. The companions return to a collapsing public world.
8. Their community service draws renewed Luminary attention.
9. Training, recovery missions, artifact conflicts, and wider war may follow.

## Disputed

The Event may be permanent, long-term, or partly reversible. Older notes lean toward permanence, but the record is not settled.

The cycle of the Event may be 3000 years. This remains an inherited claim, not an established chronology.

The final conflict may be a multi-front battle involving artifacts and allied Luminary forces. Its form remains unrecorded.
