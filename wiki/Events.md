# Events

## Summary

Events should track durable plot and history without locking the story into every legacy beat.

## Canon

- A rare cosmic or large-scale event disrupts electricity and weakens hidden protections.
- The conflict between Luminaries and dark forces becomes active in the story-present.

## Provisional Book 1 Arc

The likely Book 1 chain is: ordinary friends go trekking, a storm and river incident carries them into a weakened hidden realm, they witness an attack, return to a collapsed ordinary world, help their community, encounter a Luminary again, and are drawn into training and a wider conflict.

## Candidate

- The global electricity collapse is permanent.
- The triggering event occurs after a 3000-year cycle.
- One protagonist is captured and transformed by dark forces.
- The climax becomes a multi-front battle involving recovered artifacts and allied Luminary forces.
