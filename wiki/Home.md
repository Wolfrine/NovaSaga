# NovaSaga Lore Index

This index gathers the active records concerning the world after the [[Events|Event]], the hidden orders known as [[Factions|Luminaries]], and the darker forms that answer to fear, decay, and imbalance.

## Record Map

- [[CorePremise|The Central Record]] preserves the present account of the Event, the hidden conflict, and the ordinary lives caught between them.
- [[World]] records the visible world, hidden sanctuaries, and the changed conditions after the loss of electricity.
- [[Cosmology]] records aura, resonance, belief, and the forces that older accounts say bind mind, matter, and environment.
- [[Factions]] records hidden orders, hostile forms, older names, and uncertain classifications.
- [[Events]] records the current chronology, including the Event and the first hidden-realm breach.
- [[Artifacts]] records charged objects, relics, and tools of passage or amplification.
- [[Characters]] records persons and groups whose names, lineages, and wounds are still being recovered.
- [[Themes]] records recurring laws and moral patterns observed across the accounts.
- [[OpenQuestions]] records unsettled phenomena, variant traditions, and missing names.

## Current Reading

A concealed tradition of Luminaries exists behind ordinary history. Their work is restrained, contextual, and concerned with balance rather than domination.

The Event disrupts electricity and weakens hidden protections. Ordinary settlements face scarcity and disorder while hidden realms, older powers, and dark forces become harder to deny.

The first major human encounter appears near storm, river, and accidental passage into a weakened sanctuary. The companions matter first because of conduct under crisis, not because they begin as chosen masters of power.

Portals, extraterrestrial radiants, formal ability names, exact companion count, and later histories beyond Earth remain older variants until the central record can carry them cleanly.
