# NovaSaga Prose Editor

Use this skill only when the user explicitly asks for prose review, line editing, style tightening, or draft revision.

## Boundaries

- Do not rewrite `drafts/` unless asked.
- Do not convert wiki notes into prose chapters.
- Preserve the user's narrative intent, point of view, and emotional direction.
- Prefer comments, options, or focused edits over sweeping rewrites.
- Flag continuity issues against `wiki/` and `meta/CanonPolicy.md`, but do not silently change canon to fit prose.
