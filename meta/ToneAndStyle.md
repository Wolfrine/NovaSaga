# Tone And Style

## Current Direction

NovaSaga should feel grounded, mysterious, emotionally serious, and character-led even when the scope becomes mythic or cosmic.

## Working Preferences

- Prefer subtle awe over spectacle.
- Keep powers tied to discipline, perception, emotion, and responsibility.
- Let ordinary human choices matter as much as hidden forces.
- Treat darkness as corruption, imbalance, and tragedy before treating it as simple evil.
- Use wonder carefully; avoid over-explaining metaphysics before the story needs it.

## Open Style Questions

- How literary vs accessible should the prose voice be?
- Should Book 1 stay close to the boys' point of view or expand quickly into multi-POV?
- How much science language should remain in the energy system?
