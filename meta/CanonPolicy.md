# Canon Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines how NovaSaga material should be classified during the clean restart.

## Status Labels

- `Canon`: accepted for now and safe for future agents to rely on.
- `Provisional`: directionally useful, but still open to reshaping.
- `Candidate`: an idea extracted from archive, raw notes, or discussion that has not been adopted.
- `Open question`: a decision still unresolved.
- `Deprecated`: preserved for history but should not guide new work.

## Current Rule

Most legacy material starts as `Candidate`, not `Canon`.

Only broad foundations that recur across the archive and align with the restart direction should be marked `Canon` or `Provisional`.

## Promotion Rule

An idea can move toward canon when:

- the user explicitly approves it;
- multiple durable wiki pages depend on it without contradiction;
- it supports the core premise and tone;
- it does not force a prose rewrite the user did not request.

## Archive Rule

The archive preserves history. Do not delete it. Do not bulk-restore it. Curate from it selectively, with source notes.
