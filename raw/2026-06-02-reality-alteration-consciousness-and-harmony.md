## Common Magic

There are actual magical processes happening around humans all the time, but the human mind takes them for granted because they have become common.

A thing does not stop being magical when it becomes common. It only stops being feared.

Much of what humanity calls ordinary may be old magic successfully absorbed into habit.

Language moves thought between minds through shaped sound.

Writing lets memory leave the body and survive time.

Music changes emotional state through patterned vibration.

Medicine guides the body back toward balance.

Agriculture is a negotiation with soil, water, seed, season, and patience.

Cities exist because millions of people maintain shared belief in order, roles, roads, homes, money, law, and time.

AI or a thinking text-machine would have once been read as a spirit, oracle, demon, or magical familiar. Now it is called technology.

That does not make it less strange. It only means the mechanism became common enough to be socially accepted.
