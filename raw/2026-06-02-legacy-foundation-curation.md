## Source

## Context

NovaSaga was reset into a clean active structure. The archive preserves older generated and user-shaped lore. This note captures durable foundation concepts without bulk-restoring archive content into the active wiki.

## Key points

- NovaSaga appears centered on a global electricity collapse connected to a rare cosmic event.
- Hidden Luminary realms and sanctuaries exist behind weakened or concealed barriers.
- Luminaries are framed as subtle guardians of balance, not flashy superheroes.
- Dark beings or dark forces draw on negative energy, corruption, despair, and imbalance.
- Artifacts can amplify energy and appear morally dual-use depending on the holder.
- Book 1 has a candidate arc involving a group of boys, a trekking/river incident, a breached hidden realm, community service after collapse, Luminary training, and a larger war.
- Legacy notes include portals, extraterrestrial radiants, hidden cities, and interplanetary scope, but these are not yet stable enough for canon.

## Decisions

- Treat broad foundations as `Canon` only when repeated and structurally central.
- Treat detailed power names, ability levels, portals, extraterrestrial radiants, and exact plot beats as `Candidate` or `Provisional`.
